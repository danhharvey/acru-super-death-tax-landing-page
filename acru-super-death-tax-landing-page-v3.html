Where do I find my taxable component?

Your super is usually split into a taxable component and a tax-free component. Check your latest annual super statement or contact your super fund and ask what your taxable component is. If you have money sitting in the taxable component, that is the number to enter in the calculator.

This calculator is a simple education estimate only. Actual tax can depend on the recipient, payment pathway, taxable taxed and untaxed elements, testamentary trust use, estate planning, super fund rules and legislation at the time.
